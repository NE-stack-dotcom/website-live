Global migration : Beyond the cloud

Trapped in the cloud? 
Costs Rising?

. 


Cloud and migration are hot topics these days. It's not a "one fit for all" solution. Understanding cloud architecture is crucial to fit your business model

The future is fast approaching, and a new era of digital innovation and disruption is here. 

Our expertise in exiting cloud environments, we have mastered migrations into and between major cloud platforms like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud.

Moving data within or between cloud providers can be a challenging task, given the varying architectures, security protocols, and performance requirements each platform demands. NewEthos has consultants with years of experience this allows them to customise their approach for each migration, ensuring compatibility, compliance, and performance standards are met across platforms. 

Their deep knowledge of these ecosystems enables businesses to move between providers seamlessly, granting flexibility and operational continuity, whether it’s an initial migration into the cloud or transitioning workloads between clouds for strategic advantages.

We can also help liberate companies from cloud vendors that may be employing restrictive practices to lock in customers . 

When cloud providers become uncooperative, making it difficult for clients to move data out, NewEthos steps in with proprietary technologies designed to extract data stealthily.

Advanced bandwidth reduction and throttling methods, we can migrate data in such a way that providers remain largely unaware, avoiding potential interruptions. Beyond the cloud, we offer comprehensive solutions for NAS migrations and hypervisor transitions, including between VMware, Nutanix, Azure Stack, and Hyper-V, ensuring businesses have complete autonomy over their infrastructure with minimal risk and maximum efficiency.
 
This breadth of expertise makes Newethos a trusted ally in today’s complex and evolving data landscape.
